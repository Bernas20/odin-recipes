Bacalhau a bras

With cod, eggs and potatoes, this is one of the most popular recipes among the Portuguese. Easy and quick to make, you can easily adapt the method and try it with fish, shredded chicken or even leek.

Ingredients

  • 1 branch of Parsley
  • 800g - Potatos
  • 450g - Soaked Cod Lion
  • 1L - Cooking Oil
  • 2 c. of soup - Olive Oil
  • 4 tooth - Garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 6 Eggs
  • 12 Black Olives
  • 1 Onion

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into very thin sticks to obtain straw potatoes. Soak them in water for about half an hour to remove excess gum.
  2. Meanwhile, remove the skin and bones from the cod and shred it with your hands.
  3. Drain the potatoes and dry them well with a towel or kitchen paper. Fry them in the previously heated oil.
  4. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan and saut√© the chopped onion, peeled and cut into half moons, the garlic, peeled and chopped, and the bay leaf.
  5. When the onion and garlic start to soften, add the shredded cod and cover the pan.
  6. Cook over low heat until the cod turns white.
  7. Remove the bay leaf from the mixture and add the fried potatoes.
  8. Add the eggs, previously beaten, stir them gently, allowing them to coat all the ingredients. Don't let the eggs cook and dry out too much.
  9. Before serving, sprinkle with chopped parsley and decorate with olives.
